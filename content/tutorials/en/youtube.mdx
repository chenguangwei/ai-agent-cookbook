Previously, if we wanted to understand their video content, the most basic method was to throw a link at Gemini and ask for a summary.

Or, we could use a plugin like Youmind to summarize videos one by one, or at most, manually add interesting bits to NotebookLM one piece at a time (Youmind works similarly).

Now, something amazing has happened! The **YouTube to NotebookLM** plugin can import *all* videos from a specific creator directly into NotebookLM in one go!

Wow, I was blown away! Mom no longer has to worry about my learning!

Go search for the Chrome extension: **YouTube to NotebookLM** and install it now, then check out the feature demo below! 👇
