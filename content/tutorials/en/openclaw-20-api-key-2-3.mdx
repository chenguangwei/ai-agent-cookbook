## Step 1: Add Your Domain to Cloudflare (Free)

1. Purchase a domain on [Tencent Cloud](https://buy.cloud.tencent.com/domain?domain=ResearchWang13&tlds=.com&from=domain-console&position=overview-input-search&_t=1772266557608), choose the cheapest one. I chose a .space domain, costing only 10 RMB.
2. Go to [https://dash.cloudflare.com/](https://dash.cloudflare.com/), log in with a Google account, and choose the free plan.
3. Click the "Onboard a domain" button on the page, enter the purchased domain (e.g., ResearchWang13.com) in the input box. Note: without www or http://.
4. Click the continue button, select the free plan.
5. Cloudflare will automatically scan if your domain is bound to other IPs. Click continue.
6. Until xxxxx.ns.cloudflare.com, yyyyy.ns.cloudflare.com appear. Do not close this page.
7. Now go to the [Tencent Cloud management page](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/domain/all-domain/manage/domain-mb8bn4sq/basicinfo) to modify the domain resolution address. Click "Modify DNS Server".
8. Select "Use non-Tencent Cloud DNS", enter the domain resolution address provided by CF, and wait for successful submission.
9. Return to the Cloudflare page, scroll down, and click the "I updated my nameservers" button.
10. Skip all the following steps until your [Cloudflare page](https://dash.cloudflare.com/0e3f60448b5858ee72c3d3e03cbac4ac/home/domains) shows the domain as active.

## Final Step! Configure the Account Pool in the Browser

1. Go to the domain you purchased, mine is [ResearchWang13](https://researchwang13.space/home).
2. If you enter the setup interface, input the values according to the format in the code above. Remember to enable SSL permissions.
3. Click "Group Management" on the left, you need to create a new group for the account pool.
4. Click "Account Management" on the left, click "Add Account", name it arbitrarily, select OpenAI, choose the OAuth method.
5. Generate an authorization link, open the generated link in the server's browser, log in with your account that has already joined the business team. A token will be generated. Fill in the token, click "Complete Authorization".
6. Test: On the account management page, each account has ... on the right. Click the test link, select the model. I chose GPT - 5.3 Codex. If the test is successful, it's good.
7. Generate a dedicated API key. Click "API Keys" on the left, create a key. Note: fill the quota with 0.
