# 1. How to access ‘Claude Computer’.


The only way to access it:

1. Mac only (soon Windows, stay tuned).
2. Download the desktop app [claude.ai/download](https://claude.ai/download).
3. Use a personal plan, not a business one (I guess it’s too much compliance).
4. Upgrade your account to either Pro ($20) or Max ($100). It’s worth it.
5. Go to your Settings (bottom left), and turn on these features:

> UPDATE: They now made Claude Computer available for teams.

![Click on your name (bottom left) and then on Settings.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPddG1aAAAEglP.jpg)
![Go to Desktop app > General > Turn on Browser use > Turn on Computer use.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPdhpHaIAAZ3YU.jpg)
![When toggling on Computer use, you must accept by clicking Turn on.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPdltUbkAA3Wsa.jpg)
![You can deny apps. I denied my 1Password (it has my passwords).](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPdquZXYAAZw_K.jpg)
![I also clicked on Accessibility and Screen recording to grant Claude access.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPdvEuWcAA8U1P.jpg)

Once done, connect your Computer to your phone with Claude Dispatch:

1. Go to your left bar menu on the Claude desktop app.
2. Click on Dispatch. And follow these steps:

![Click on Dispatch. It’s only available on the desktop app for paid personal accounts.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPd0DTbwAAPGkC.jpg)
![Click on Get started.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPd4xuXMAAki0s.jpg)
![Scan this QR code with your phone. Make sure you have the Claude app on your phone, too. And connected to the same account. It’s free.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPd8sFW0AAYJIA.jpg)
![I turned on every setting to test it all. Then click Finish setup.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPeA59asAAMkm6.jpg)
![This is what my computer sees.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPeE9jaUAA0AHw.jpg)
![This is what my phone sees. The same thing.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPeK8UbwAE9_0r.jpg)

You are all set. Time to test it now.

# 2. My favorite use cases of ‘Claude Computer’.


A reminder on what’s needed to use Claude Computer, with your phone:

1. You must have the Claude desktop app + a paid personal account.
2. Your computer must always be turned on. That’s why people buy Mac minis.
3. Go to your phone. Open the Claude app. Go to Dispatch. Ask for something:

![My first prompt: go to my dashboard. Give me some data.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPeUNyaoAAGBL7.jpg)

Now let’s do some harder tasks:

## Find a freelancer for me.


Prompt:

> Go to my Fiverr account on Chrome. Post a job offer for [task]. Then, send a message to the best 10 [job needed] for [task]. Check if I have any unanswered DM, and follow up until one accepts the [task] for [budget] maximum.

![I did it on my phone. Then Claude Dispatch asked me questions.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPemJqXoAAOmN-.jpg)
![It’s so satisfying to be outside and read a text from your Claude, working.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPeqUjXQAAEjap.jpg)
![Sometimes you do need to be direct about wanting everything done without permission.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPevyaXoAABnB7.jpg)
![And it worked. I keep receiving inbounds from Fiverr now.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPe0PyXIAAwZWJ.jpg)
![4 offers in a few hours. I am outside on my phone.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPe5F8XYAAvrz8.jpg)

## Find viral ads from today.


Prompt:

> Go to the Meta Ads Library: search the latest viral ads about [topic] in [country] only. Create a Google Sheets with at least 50 links to 50 ads that mention [topic].

Here is what I asked my own Claude Dispatch:

> Go to the Meta Ads Library: search the latest viral ads about Claude or Anthropic in the U.S. only. Create a Google Sheets with at least 10 links to 10 ads that mention Claude or Anthropic.

![And it worked. Now imagine scheduling this every morning? More after this section on how to schedule daily tasks.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPfBA9bcAAkHRp.jpg)

And you can build up on it:

> Now go to Gemini and recreate the ad. But make it about subscribing to my newsletter "How to AI". Each time you generate it on Gemini, I want you to save them on my Canva account in a new folder "Meta Ads".

Sky is the limit. Actually, you are the limit.

# 3. How to schedule tasks with Claude.


That was the obvious next step: scheduling a task with Claude.

Just like… an employee reporting to you.

1. Go to the left sidebar, and look for Scheduled.
2. Create a New task. Give it a name and description.
3. The most important part: the prompt. Test it before.
4. Choose a frequency. And make sure to select the right model & folder.

![Start here.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPfJltbIAAsRLF.jpg)
![Give it a name and a description. Try a prompt on the normal chat before pasting it here.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPfRBlXIAA_j6l.jpg)
![I made myself a solid prompt to go through the daily AI papers from arXiv.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPfVMtWgAArLpl.jpg)
![Make sure to select the right model after clicking More options > and pick your Cowork folder as I explained before.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPffh_W8AA4AQM.jpg)
![You are good to go, as long as your computer is “awake”.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEPfmuMbAAA93mu.jpg)
